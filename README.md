# Credit-Card-Customer-Churn-and-Retention-Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes credit card customer behavior to identify churn drivers, evaluate customer engagement, and uncover revenue-generating customer segments. Using **SQL Server, SQL, Power BI, and DAX**, the analysis delivers actionable insights to support customer retention strategies, improve business performance, and enable data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Financial institutions face challenges in retaining customers and maximizing revenue. This project aims to answer the following business questions:

* What is the current customer churn rate?
* Which customer segments are most likely to churn?
* Which card categories generate the highest transaction volume?
* How do customer demographics and engagement impact churn?
* Which customers contribute the most to business revenue?

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Customer inactivity is strongly associated with higher churn risk.
* Blue Card customers contribute the highest share of transaction volume.
* High-value customer segments generate a significant portion of overall revenue.
* Customer engagement metrics can help identify churn-prone customers early.
* Churn analysis highlights opportunities for targeted retention strategies.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Launch targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
* Re-engage inactive customers through personalized offers and loyalty programs.
* Focus retention efforts on high-value customer segments.
* Improve customer experience for card categories with higher churn rates.
* Leverage customer behavior indicators to proactively reduce churn.
